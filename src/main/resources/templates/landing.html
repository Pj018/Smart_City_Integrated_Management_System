Platform Features

Emergency SOS Reporting

Instantly report critical incidents with prioritized escalation to emergency responders.

Real-Time Complaint Tracking

Track the live status of your reported civic issues from submission to complete resolution.

AI-Based Predictive Alerts

Leverage machine learning to predict and prevent urban infrastructure failures.

Geo-Location Mapping

Accurate GPS pin-drops ensure city staff always navigate to the exact incident location.

Transparent Public Dashboard

View city-wide analytics, department performance, and ongoing maintenance transparency.

Earn civic points for active community reporting to redeem local government incentives.

How It Works

Step 1

Citizen Raises Complaint

Users capture photos, add details, and submit geo-tagged issues instantly via the portal.

Step 2

Admin Assigns to Staff

The central control hub maps the issue to the relevant department and dispatches field workers.

Step 3

Issue Resolved & Citizen Notified

Staff mark the issue resolved with proof, and the citizen receives an automated success alert.
